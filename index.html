What is Personal Branding?

Personal branding is the intentional practice of shaping how the world perceives you-online and off. It is not about being famous. It is about being known, trusted, and sought-after by exactly the right people.

Think of it this way: your brand already exists. Every LinkedIn post, every Instagram bio, every time someone Googles your name-an impression is being formed. The only question is whether you are the one deliberately shaping it.

  • Your name, face, and expertise positioned as a recognisable trusted asset
  • Consistent messaging across LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and X
  • A reputation that makes opportunities come to you
  • The difference between being discovered and being overlooked
02
⚠

Common Mistakes I See Every Day

After working with 10+ brands across industries and platforms, I have seen the same patterns hold talented people back again and again. Here is what most people get wrong.

  • Posting without positioning: content with no clear who I am and who I serve
  • Being everywhere, standing for nothing: spreading thin across every platform without mastering one
  • Copying others in your niche: blending in instead of owning what makes you different
  • Inconsistency: posting for two weeks, disappearing for two months
  • Waiting to be ready: perfectionism that quietly kills all momentum
  • Chasing follower counts: vanity metrics instead of real trust and conversion
